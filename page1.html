Our Game


You are an alien named Cleitus and you are from the planet 'Khronos'. Your spaceship has crash landed on Earth. Your ship is broken and pieces of it have been scattered around Earth. Your stranded on planet ('Earth'), where you know nothing about and your mission is to return back home, to planet 'Khronos'.

To return home you must find and collect all the pieces from the crash in order to fix the ship. With limited resources and allies, you must survive and return home. As you play the game, you may come across some humans who will aid you in your mission. You will have the ability to trade weapons with them or hire the humans. Cleitus has a base where he can build a spaceship and buy/upgrade weapons.

Collect Research Points

You can gain research points when you kill Humans or complete a mission or find parts for the ship. You can use RP for many things in this game such as you can purchase weapons and armory.

Meet new Friends

There are chances to interact with NPCs for trade and story quests. If you earn their respect then they will reward you with new weapons!

Multiple types of Enemies

Humans are the enemies in this game. The Humans abilities to attack you are level capped based on the location and level. The Enemies come in waves with the game getting increasingly difficult with each level.

Randomised Loot drops

The will be randomised loot - Humans will drop random loot inline with your players current level. weapon drops are (country specific) and upgraded in the store.
